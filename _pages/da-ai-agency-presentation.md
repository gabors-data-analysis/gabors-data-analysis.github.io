## Case Study 

### data

- **Employee Commits Dataset** (Case Study Data)  
  [github.com/gabors-data-analysis/da-w-ai/tree/main/data/employee_commits](https://github.com/gabors-data-analysis/da-w-ai/tree/main/data/employee_commits)

Prompt

Here is a dataset on employees at a single company:  

* *x*: is number of days with the company
* *y*: is number of monthly commits (to any repo at company). 
* *i*: is an employee in all IT and analytics divisions

Uncover the pattern. Return a single chart and one paragraph management summary.
